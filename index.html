Environmental Sustainability

What is environmental sustainability? Environmental sustainability is the responsibility to conserve natural resources and protect global ecosystems to support health and wellbeing, now and in the future.

Why is it important? There are so many reasons! Primarily, our concern is that we are using up the resources on our planet faster than we can replenish them.
